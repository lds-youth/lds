Multiple youth in your family attending?

The registration form only allows one submission per email sign in. So if you have multiple youth attending youth conference, you will need to sign in to one email account, submit the form and then sign out. Then, sign back using a different email account for the next child. We realize that this is inconvenient, but since google forms doesn't currently have a good way to submit a handwritten signature using a signature pad, we have to create an electronic signature using unique email sign-ins for each submission. Thanks for your patience!
